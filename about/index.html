About Us

Founded in 2015, Warwick Irish Society was set up in order to promote Irish culture within Warwick University

History

We are the Warwick Irish Society, one of the newest societies on campus! If you have an Irish background, are interested in Irish culture or just want to have some great craic, then this is the society for you!

We were only founded in 2015 and already our exec are working on some great events for this year and the next. We hold regular social events including; circling before club nights, talks from influential Irish speakers, and socials for big Irish events like St. Patrick's Day!
